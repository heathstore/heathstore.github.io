In Individual medley events, you swim all the strokes in one race in a certain order. It is very hard to excel in, because you have to be good in all the strokes, and you have to be able to sprint each time you change strokes. The distances for this event are the 100, 200, and 400 meter/yard individual medley.

Individual Medley Strokes
Stroke Order
Butterfly First
Backstroke Second
Breaststroke Third
Freestyle Fourth
