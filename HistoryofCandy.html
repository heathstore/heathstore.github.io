History of Candy


The first candy dates back in the aincient times when people snacked on honey straight from the bees.
But candy would never of been started unless people had started rolling nuts and fruit in honey. Candy manufacturing began in the middle ages, But back then sugar was very expensive and only the rich could afford it. In the seventeenth century candy was cheaper and became popular.
By the mid-1800s there were over 400 candy factories in the U.S.A
