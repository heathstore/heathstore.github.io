Freestyle is a stroke that most people recognize. You use a flutter kick and a circular pull, pulling with one arm at a time. Freestyle is one of the most commonly recognized strokes, and the most commonly swam stroke. It has the most events. A lower version of freestyle is doggy paddle. The events in which you swim freestyle are the 50, 100, 200, 400/500, 800/1000, and the 1500/1650 meter/yard freestyle. However, in the individual medley events, where you swim all strokes in one race,you also have to swim freestyle
