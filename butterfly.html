In the stroke butterfly, you use a dolphin kick and a doubled arm stroke. You also use a 1-2 stroke-kick ratio. Many swimmers believe that butterfly is the hardest of all the strokes. You can breath whenever you want in butterfly, but most people take 2-3 pulls then one breath, although some Olympians breath every stroke. The events in which you swim butterfly are the 50, 100, and 200 meter/yard butterfly and the individual medley events.
