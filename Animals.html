All about pandas

Pandas, also known as Ailuropoda melanoleuca, Ursidae, Carnivora
are large furry large animals. Panda Bears have large hard teeth and
claws that they use to rip the bamboo from the tree. Pandas have
a large white furry body with black spots around their arms,
legs, eyes, and ears. The pandas weigh an average of to 230 pounds.

Where pandas live

Giant pandas live in a some places in China, such as:
the places of Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Gansu. They once lived in lowland
areas, but developing projects such as cutting down trees

What pandas eat

A wild giant pandas diet 99% bamboo. Pandas in zoos eat more diverse diets
The other 1% consists of other grasses and small animals. Such as fawns.
In zoos, giant pandas eat bamboo, rice, a sort of gruel,
high-fiber biscuits, and miscellaneous vegetables
