Pandas are from the Domain Eukarya; Animal Kingdom; Chordata Phylum;
Class Mammalia; Order Carnivora; Family Ursidae;
Genus Ailuropoda; Species Ailuropoda melanoleuca
Pandas walk with their paws turned inward. Pandas cannot support their body
on their hind legs which is why they cannot stand on their hind legs. They
also have a fake thumb that corresponds to out fifth finger, and they have a
sixth finger after it. They also have an opposable pseudothumb that
is their first finger.

Pandas have thick black and white fur that is very soft and fluffy
Pandas do not have this fur when they are born, they are pink and hairless.
They are also very vulnerable, because they are the size of a thumb.
